Noodles

Description

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough that is rolled flat and cut into long strips or strings. They are typically boiled in water and can be served with a variety of sauces, vegetables, and proteins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • Your choice of sauce and toppings

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add eggs.
  2. Gradually mix flour into the eggs until a dough forms. Knead for 5-10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness and cut into strips.
  5. Boil water in a large pot. Add noodles and cook for 3-5 minutes until al dente.
  6. Drain and toss with olive oil, sauce, and toppings of your choice.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
